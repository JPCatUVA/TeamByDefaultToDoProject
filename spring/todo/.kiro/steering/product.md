# Product Overview

This is a full-stack, secure **To-Do application** built by Team By Default as a Revature training project.  

## Business Objectives
- To create a more enjoyable user experience for anyone used to using their boring run of the mill task managers.

## Target Users
- Our application should be general enough to be convenient for a wide base of varied users who wish to track tasks, edit them, and eliminate them as they or their subtasks are completed.

## Product Purpose
-We would like our users to effeciently and conveniently be able to track adjenda items they may need to complete on a daily, monthly or yearly basis.
